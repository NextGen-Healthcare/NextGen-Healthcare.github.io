Connecting the Next Generation of Healthcare Infrastructure Professionals

A multi‑disciplinary community of young professionals building, operating, and improving healthcare estates across the public and private sectors.

Join the Network See Upcoming Events

Who we are

We bring together early to mid‑career professionals (5–15 years’ experience) in healthcare construction, development, estates, FM, sustainability, and digital—creating a supportive space to learn, connect, and collaborate.

What we do

We run informal Coffee & Catch‑Ups, IHEEM gatherings, cross‑network All Network Sessions, and After Work Drinks. We also host Special Interest Groups (SIGs) to deep‑dive on topics like Energy & Sustainability, Compliance, and Regulations.
