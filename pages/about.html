About NextGen

Building the future of healthcare infrastructure through collaboration, innovation, and shared expertise.

Our Story

NextGen Healthcare Network was founded by young professionals who recognised the need for a dedicated platform connecting early to mid‑career individuals in the healthcare infrastructure sector.

We saw a gap—while established professionals had networks, younger professionals lacked a space to connect, share experiences, and learn from peers facing similar challenges. Our network bridges this gap, creating opportunities for meaningful connections and professional growth.

Today, we represent members across the UK, spanning roles in healthcare construction, facilities management, project development, and strategic planning across NHS trusts, private providers, construction firms, consultancies, and government bodies.

Mission & Values

Our Mission

To connect, support, and empower the next generation of healthcare infrastructure professionals, fostering innovation and excellence in the design, construction, and management of healthcare facilities across the UK.

Our Values

  • Collaboration: Working together to achieve more.
  • Innovation: Embracing new ideas and technologies.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards.
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming all perspectives.
  • Growth: Continuous learning and development.
