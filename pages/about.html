Who We Are

NextGen Healthcare Network (NGHN) is a cross-sector professional community bringing together early to mid-career professionals working across healthcare infrastructure in the UK.

Our members come from diverse backgrounds spanning estates and facilities management, engineering, sustainability, digital innovation, clinical-adjacent roles, policy, procurement, and strategic planning.

What unites us is a shared commitment to improving healthcare delivery through better infrastructure, innovative thinking, and collaborative problem-solving.

Why We Exist

Healthcare infrastructure is at a crossroads. The sector faces unprecedented challenges: aging estates, sustainability targets, digital transformation, and evolving clinical needs. Yet too often, the professionals working to solve these challenges operate in silosâ€”separated by discipline, organization, or geography.

NGHN exists to break down these barriers. We create opportunities for emerging leaders to:

  • Connect across traditional professional boundaries
  • Share knowledge and best practices
  • Learn from experienced practitioners and each other
  • Influence the future direction of healthcare infrastructure
  • Build lasting professional relationships

By bringing together diverse perspectives, we accelerate innovation and create a stronger, more resilient healthcare infrastructure sector.
