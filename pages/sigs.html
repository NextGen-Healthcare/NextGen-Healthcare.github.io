Special Interest Groups

Join focused communities within our network to dive deep into specific areas of healthcare infrastructure.

What are SIGs?

Special Interest Groups (SIGs) bring together professionals with shared interests. Each SIG runs talks, roundtables, and projects relevant to its focus.

Monthly Meetings

Virtual and in-person gatherings to discuss developments.

Expert Speakers

Access to industry leaders and subject matter experts.

Collaborative Projects

Tackle real-world challenges and share best practice.

Current SIGs

Energy & Sustainability

Energy solutions, BMS & controls, retrofit and decarbonisation.

Compliance & Regulations

HTM/HBN, Building Regs, safety cases and governance.

Innovation Hub

AI, IoT, and smart building systems for healthcare estates.

Design & Construction

Best practice in healthcare facility design and delivery.

Patient Experience

Environments that improve experience and outcomes.

Future Leaders

Career development and leadership for emerging talent.

How to Join a SIG

1

Become a Network Member

Join the NextGen Healthcare Network by completing our membership form. Membership is free for eligible professionals.

2

Choose Your SIGs

Select up to three SIGs that align with your interests and expertise. You can change your selections at any time.

3

Get Involved

Attend meetings, contribute to discussions, and collaborate on projects. Active members can also apply to become SIG leaders.
