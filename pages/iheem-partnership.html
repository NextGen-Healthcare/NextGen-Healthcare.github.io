Partnership Overview

The NextGen Healthcare Network (NGHN) is proud to partner with the Institute of Healthcare Engineering and Estate Management (IHEEM), the UK's leading professional body for healthcare estates and engineering professionals.

This partnership represents a shared commitment to professional development, knowledge exchange, and cultivating future leadership in healthcare estates and infrastructure. Through this collaboration, we aim to strengthen the pipeline of skilled professionals entering and progressing within the healthcare infrastructure sector.

Both organisations recognise the critical importance of supporting early- and mid-career professionals as they navigate their careers and contribute to improving healthcare delivery across the UK.

About IHEEM

The Institute of Healthcare Engineering and Estate Management (IHEEM) is the professional body representing those who manage, plan, design, construct, and maintain healthcare facilities and associated engineering services across the UK and internationally.

With a membership spanning NHS trusts, private healthcare providers, consultancies, contractors, and suppliers, IHEEM plays a vital role in setting professional standards, providing training and development opportunities, and advocating for excellence in healthcare estates management.

For NGHN members working in estates, facilities, engineering, sustainability, and related fields, IHEEM represents a natural progression in professional development‚Äîoffering chartered status pathways, technical guidance, and networking opportunities with established professionals.

Partnership Objectives

Our partnership is built on shared objectives that benefit both organisations and, most importantly, the individuals we serve.

Supporting the Next Generation

Create accessible pathways for early-career professionals to engage with established institutions and professional development frameworks.

Raising Sector Visibility

Improve awareness and understanding of careers in healthcare estates and engineering among emerging professionals and potential sector entrants.

Cross-Sector Collaboration

Foster connections between different disciplines, organisations, and career stages to strengthen the sector as a whole.

Knowledge Exchange

Facilitate the sharing of expertise, research, and best practices between NGHN members and IHEEM's established professional community.

Professional Pathways

Create clear connections between early networking and engagement (NGHN) and formal professional membership and development (IHEEM).

Sector Leadership

Develop the future leaders who will shape healthcare infrastructure policy, innovation, and delivery across the UK.

Relationship & Independence

While we are proud to partner with IHEEM, it is important to note that NGHN remains an independent network. The partnership is collaborative and mutually supportive, designed to benefit professionals at all stages of their careers.

Membership of NGHN does not require or imply IHEEM membership, and vice versa. NGHN welcomes members from all professional backgrounds across healthcare infrastructure, whether or not they are affiliated with IHEEM or any other professional body.

This partnership reflects a shared commitment to developing the sector, but each organisation retains its own identity, governance, and priorities.
