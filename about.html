About the Network

We exist to connect, upskill, and celebrate young professionals shaping the future of healthcare infrastructure.

Our story

The NextGen Healthcare Network emerged from a simple idea: create a space where early to mid‑career professionals can learn from each other and build meaningful relationships across disciplines. We bring together people working in design, construction, asset management, estates & facilities, sustainability, technology, and clinical support.

Mission & values

  • Connection: build supportive cross‑sector relationships.
  • Learning: share best practice, resources, and lived experience.
  • Action: translate insight into better, safer, and more sustainable estates.
  • Inclusion: create welcoming spaces for all backgrounds.

Who it’s for

Young professionals with roughly 5–15 years’ experience across the public and private sectors. Students and career‑changers with a genuine interest in healthcare estates are also welcome.
